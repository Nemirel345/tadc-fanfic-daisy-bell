Chapter 3

Toddler Trouble in the Tent

Pomni: Come on, Dee. You’ve got this.

Pomni sits on the floor with her arms stretched wide.

Daisy plants both paws, rocks once, twice, then face-plants into the mat. She lets out a tiny huff, then tries again.

Jax hovers behind her, arms ready to catch, but pretends he won’t.

Jax: Form needs work. Ten out of ten for effort, though.

Daisy bunches up, wiggles, and manages to scoot forward an inch.

Pomni: There you go, keep coming.

Daisy lunges, shuffles two more scoots, and manages to crawl for exactly four feet.

Pomni beams as Daisy keeps moving.

Daisy barrels into Pomni’s hands. She wobbles like she’s about to topple, but Jax’s arms are already there, just in case, not quite touching.

Pomni laughs.

Pomni: That’s my girl. Look at you!

Jax: Round two?

Pomni resets Daisy on the mat, then scoots behind to spot. Jax sits down a little further in front.

Jax: Finish line’s this way, Dee.

Daisy wobbles for a second, then commits.

Caine drops in unannounced.

Caine: GLORIOUS GREETINGS, MY GALLANT GEARHEADS! It’s time for another adventure!

Pomni and Jax exchange a stare.

Pomni: Caine! We’re already in an adventure, remember?

Caine: What adventure?

Jax lifts the toddler.

Caine: Ah, yes. I… totally didn’t forget about the long-term adventure. Nevertheless, double adventure today. Delightful! Meet me at the stage.

Caine disappears as quickly as he came in.

Daisy squeaks and tucks closer to Jax.

Pomni: What do you think?

Jax: We can’t really say no to Caine, he’d just snap-summon us.

Pomni: Well, we also can’t really leave Daisy in here alone. Besides… I’m kinda curious what Caine has in store today.

Jax: I’ll go see if Zoobs is done with the stroller.

Jax exits, goes over, and knocks at Zooble’s door with Daisy in his arms.

The door cracks open.

Zooble: What do you want, Jax?

Jax: Relax, truce. We need the stroller. Caine’s got a new adventure and we’re bringing her.

Zooble opens wider. The stroller sits finished in the corner.

Zooble: It’s been ready for days. You two haven’t left Pomni’s room in a week.

Daisy spots Zooble’s colourful chestpiece and reaches for it.

Zooble’s posture softens just a bit. They unclip it and hand it to her.

Zooble: She got big fast.

Jax: Speedrun, baby. Any% crawl.

Zooble: Cute.

Jax: Terrifying.

Jax steps in, eyes the build, and gives the handle a test push.

Jax: This thing safe?

Zooble: Safer than you.

Jax: Why does some of this look like it comes from your parts box?

Zooble: The instructions were… unhelpful. I had to improvise.

Jax mumbles under his breath.

Jax: Thanks… for this.

Zooble: What was that? Couldn’t make it out.

Jax: Nothing. Never mind.

Zooble: Well, in any case, I didn’t do it for you.

Jax: Didn’t say you were.

Zooble nods at the harness.

Zooble: Put her in. I’m checking fit once, then you’re out of my door.

Jax sets Daisy into the seat. She kicks, squeaks, then settles when the last buckle clips. Zooble adjusts the strap.

Zooble: Okay, she’s good.

Jax: Yeah… she is.

Jax turns the stroller toward the hall.

Jax: We’ll bring it back in one piece.

Zooble: Bring her back in one piece.

Jax stops and meets their eyes.

Jax: That’s the plan.

Jax pushes the stroller to the stage. Pomni’s already there with Ragatha, Kinger, and Gangle. Zooble trails a few steps behind, arms crossed, pretending they aren’t with him.

Caine appears from nowhere.

Caine: Today’s adventure is: Clockwork Citadel Siege!

The title appears in floating bold letters behind him.

Caine: The Gasket-Masked Marauders are invading the citadel. You must use steam-powered weaponry to-

Pomni: Caine! We have a toddler!

Jax: Yeah… we’re not marching into a siege.

Caine freezes mid-exposition, then ponders.

Caine: Hmmm, how about I tweak the adventure slightly? Make it more… child-appropriate.

Jax: Yes! Or else we sit it out.

Caine: Excellent then!

Caine materializes a coloured marker and scribbles out the Siege from the floating title, replacing it with Stroll.

Caine: I’ve adjusted the hostility down… to 5.7%.

Jax: Lose the hostiles.

Caine: To five?

Pomni: To none.

Caine: Fine… zero it is. You’ll enjoy a casual walk in a totally non-hostile citadel. Strollers welcome.

Ragatha: That… doesn’t sound too bad, actually.

Caine flicks his cane. The portal opens. They each step through.

The citadel shifts from a gloomy evening to a bright sunny day.

A line of Marauders stand at the entrance of the citadel, holding pamphlets.

Jax nudges the stroller forward with Pomni at his side. Ragatha gathers a guide from one of the Marauders, then returns. She flips through it.

Ragatha: Looks like they have a botanical garden.

Pomni: Maybe there’s interesting bugs in there.

Kinger: An insect collection?

Ragatha: Maybe. We’ll regroup later, okay?

Ragatha accompanies Kinger toward the gardens.

Zooble: Workshop walkthrough?

Zooble tilts their head to Gangle.

Gangle: It’s a tour.

Zooble: Sure, that sounds fun.

Jax stares at the Marauders.

Jax: Think those mouth-vents used to shoot fire?

Pomni: Not today, they don’t.

They find a fenced patch of grass. It’s soft, a little too green. A fountain gently spews water in the center.

Jax spreads the blanket. Pomni passes him a snack. Daisy kicks and squeaks, clearly wanting off the stroller.

Jax: Okay, pit stop.

Pomni: Field trip picnic.

Jax locks the stroller brakes with his foot. Pomni unbuckles Daisy and sets her down on the blanket.

Daisy immediately reaches for the grass. Jax plucks a blade from her paw before it reaches her mouth.

Jax: Nice try, herbivore.

Pomni smothers a laugh and pulls out a toy.

Pomni: Here, chew this fuzzy… thing. What even is this supposed to be?

Jax: At least it isn’t a stuffed corn plush.

The clock tower’s shadow moves slightly, then snaps back to noon.

Pomni: This is… actually nice.

Jax: For now, at least. I can’t believe Caine made such a cool adventure, and we’re ignoring most of the action.

Jax looks at Daisy.

Jax: Worth it though.

Jax leans back, watching Daisy.

Jax: Stay within two feet of the blanket, delinquent.

Pomni looks toward the promenade where Kinger and Ragatha disappeared.

Pomni: Ten minutes and we join back with the others.

Jax: Make it fifteen. She’s finally not screaming.

Daisy crawls toward Jax. He picks her up and sets her on his lap.

Pomni smiles.

Pomni: Okay, fifteen.

A hand-painted sign points down a catwalk: WORKSHOP WALKTHROUGH. Zooble and Gangle approach. A guide welcomes them, then steps aside to let them pass.

The catwalk overlooks active bays. Artisans assemble intricate devices. One of them picks their device like a rifle, aims… and sprays bubbles.

Gangle’s ribbon loops around Zooble’s wrist. Loose. Happy.

Zooble: Public.

The ribbon loosens, then squeezes before slipping away.

They move on to a mezzanine. Gangle lays her mask onto Zooble’s shoulder for exactly one second. Zooble’s cheeks gain colour. They look aside, their fingers find ribbon and press once.

A chime rings from the clock tower.

Zooble: Meet back with the others?

Gangle: One more bay.

Zooble: Fine.

They walk on, Gangle close enough to brush, Zooble pretending they’re not. Both happy.

Glass doors open to the conservatory. It’s bright, busy. Ragatha and Kinger wander in.

Ragatha: Oh, look! Butterflies!

Ragatha leads Kinger into a shaded wing. Several mechanical insects flutter about the garden’s flowers.

Kinger: Hmm, Hesperiidae.

Ragatha: Hespe… what?

Kinger: Skippers!

Ragatha: Oh… nice.

Ragatha kneels down to the flower bed. Kinger walks just a bit further.

Ragatha: You should come smell the lavender.

Kinger: Oh, wow! They have Dynastinae down here.

Ragatha looks at him, wide-eyed.

Kinger: Rhinoceros beetles.

Ragatha: You… really know your stuff.

Kinger: I guess so. I mean, these are pretty accurate for clockwork facsimiles.

Ragatha: Caine really outdid himself on this one.

The clock tower chimes outside.

Ragatha: We should regroup soon.

A moth perches itself on Kinger’s shoulder.

Ragatha: Oh, it seems you’ve made a new friend.

Kinger: Sphingidae, hawk moth.

Kinger gently places it on a nearby branch.

Kinger: Sorry, little one. You can’t go out with me.

Ragatha: There you are!

Ragatha spots Pomni and Jax sitting in the grass. She lifts her dress slightly and hurries toward them. Kinger follows.

Ragatha sits on the edge of the blanket.

Ragatha: How’s our little crawler?

Jax: She’s a menace, but at least the volume is tolerable today.

Pomni: How were the gardens?

Kinger: They had mechanical bugs!

Ragatha: And the flowers looked nice, too.

Daisy crawls toward Ragatha. She picks her up and Daisy immediately starts grabbing at Ragatha’s licorice hair.

Ragatha: Easy there, I need that.

Jax: Told you.

Pomni leans back on her hands.

Pomni: Sounds like you two had fun. Maybe even more than us.

Jax: Speak for yourself! We had fifteen solid minutes of no screaming. That’s luxury.

Daisy twists in Ragatha’s arms. Her ears perk up. Pomni follows her gaze.

Pomni: Oh, there they are.

Gangle and Zooble walk up the path, close. Gangle’s ribbon is loosely looped around Zooble’s wrist, swinging between them.

They’re almost at the blanket when Gangle notices the others looking. The ribbon drops like it was never there.

Pomni pretends to readjust the blanket. Ragatha looks away.

Gangle: We found the workshop!

Zooble: It was… interesting.

Pomni: Glad you two had fun.

Jax’s ears tilt forward. He smirks.

Jax: So… just friends, huh?

Zooble: Oh FUCK right off, Jax. We are just friends.

Gangle: Yep! Just… normal… very friendly… friends.

Jax: Sure.

Ragatha gives him a light nudge with her elbow.

Jax: Hey! I didn’t say it was a bad thing.

Zooble’s gaze flicks around the circle. Nobody else pushes. They sigh and sit at the edge of the blanket. Gangle settles next to them.

Daisy reaches out. Ragatha hands her off to Zooble.

Zooble: So… stroller’s still in one piece?

Jax: No user error yet, Zoobie.

Ragatha looks around the circle.

Ragatha: This is nice.

Jax: Don’t jinx it.

Pomni smiles and looks at Daisy.

Pomni: Yeah… I could get used to this. I’m… glad we’re doing it…

Pomni looks around the circle, then back at Daisy.

Pomni: Together.
