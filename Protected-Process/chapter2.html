Chapter 2

Small Glitches, Big Feelings

Jax: Why won’t she stop? Make her stop!

Pomni holds Daisy in her arms, gently rocking her back and forth.

Pomni: I’m trying! I’m trying. You got the milk?

Jax shakes the bottle again and tests it on his wrist.

Jax: Still too warm.

Pomni: Damn.

A knock at the door. Ragatha’s voice across.

Ragatha: Guys, I brought you something to drink.

She pushes the door open.

Pomni: Please tell us it’s lukewarm milk. Please!

Ragatha: I’m sorry, Pomni, all I brought is tea for you two. I can go get some if you need.

Jax: What we need is a pause button.

Ragatha looks at both of them.

Ragatha: You guys look… exhausted.

Jax: We haven’t slept in three days, or was it four?

Pomni: Feels like eight!

Jax: The clock jumped two minutes while I was staring at it. That was fun.

Ragatha: You two need some sleep. Maybe… I could take care of Daisy while you get some rest.

Pomni: Oh, that would be phenomenal! Here, take her. Take. Her.

Pomni hands off the child burrito. Jax adds the bottle of milk.

Ragatha: Oh… okay! I’m gonna do my best here. Maybe I’ll just… and you’re both asleep already. Good. Hey there, little one. Auntie Ragatha is taking care of you now.

She starts humming. The crying softens.

Pomni blinks awake. Jax sits up, ears a mess.

Ragatha hums off-key in the chair. Daisy is sound asleep. Three empty bottles line the table. The tea is cold.

Jax: Rags? How long have we-

Ragatha: Four hours.

Pomni: Are you… okay?

Ragatha: Oh, we’re great. Totally fine!

Her eye twitches.

Pomni: You didn’t have to, you know. Thanks.

Ragatha: It’s nothing, really. Easy… so easy.

Jax reaches out.

Jax: I’ll take her.

Ragatha: I’ve got her!

Pomni sits up fully. Jax swings his legs off the bed.

Jax: You’ve had her for four hours, Rags. You’ve done more than enough.

Ragatha: I’m fine. Really. We’re great.

Her eye twitches again.

Jax: Yeah, well, let us be great for a bit. Hand her over.

Ragatha hesitates, then carefully passes Daisy back to Pomni.

Daisy gives a tiny squeak, ears flicking.

Jax: Oh no. Round two.

Pomni adjusts her hold.

Pomni: Shhhhh.

Ragatha: Okay, just… support the head. She likes when you sway a little.

Pomni nods. The baby curls in against her without waking. Her paw flexes once against Pomni’s chest.

Jax glances at Ragatha’s tea.

Jax: That’s gone cold.

Ragatha: I… didn’t really have a hand free.

She brushes at a faint spit-up stain on her dress, as if she just noticed it.

Pomni: You’ve been amazing, Rags. Go lie down before you fall over.

Ragatha: I’m not going to-

She reaches for the arm of the chair, missing by an inch. She catches herself on the second try.

Ragatha: Okay… maybe a tiny nap.

Jax: We’ll wake you up if she starts a revolution.

Ragatha stands up, smoothing her dress. She slips out, gently closing the door behind her.

Pomni looks down at the bundle in her arms. Daisy’s ear gives a twitch, as if she’s dreaming.

Jax picks up the open pamphlet on the table. He flips it to a page covered in little icons.

Jax: All right, Dee. Let’s see what we’re doing wrong today.

He squints.

Jax: Sleep when the baby sleeps. Too late for that one.

Pomni snorts softly.

Pomni: We actually did, for once.

Jax: Some crying is normal. Define some.

Daisy jerks in her sleep, nose scrunching like she’s about to start up again. Pomni rocks her a little, slow, side to side. The lights above flicker once, then steadies.

Pomni: Shh… It’s okay. You’re okay. We’re right here.

Daisy lets out one tiny whine, then settles.

Jax: Hiccups, twitching, weird little faces are all normal.

Pomni: She’s allowed to make weird faces. She’s… learning what existing feels like.

Jax: Yeah. Same honestly.

Jax lets the pamphlet drop down against his leg, watching Daisy’s ears relax.

Jax: You think… Rags will actually sleep?

Pomni: If she doesn’t, I’ll make her! She did great, though.

Jax: Yeah, she did… but don’t you dare tell her I said that, or anyone.

Pomni watches him for a moment. His ears are relaxed, tail not wagging.

Pomni: Jax?

Jax: Yeah?

She hesitates, then goes for it.

Pomni: What’s… up with you?

Jax: What do you mean?

She shifts Daisy in her arms, careful not to wake her up.

Pomni: You’re… less of an ASSHOLE than usual.

Jax: Wow. Glowing review.

Pomni: I’m serious.

She keeps her voice low.

Pomni: You let Ragatha help. You read the pamphlet. You haven’t called her an it for hours.

Jax looks away.

Jax: Yeah, well… she grabbed my finger. That’s a binding contract.

Pomni: That’s not how contracts work.

Jax: It is in here.

He taps his chest with the edge of the pamphlet.

Pomni studies him.

Pomni: So… what changed?

Jax looks at the ceiling for a second, searching for something that doesn’t sound like a confession.

Jax: I dunno.

He shrugs, lazy and a little forced.

Jax: You ever try to be a full-time jerk around a baby? Kinda hard when they only know like… three things. Crying, grabbing stuff, and… this.

He nods at Daisy.

Pomni: This what?

Jax: Existing at you.

Pomni: Existing at you?

Jax: Yeah. She doesn’t know I’m supposed to be awful. Feels… dirty to introduce that on purpose, I guess.

Pomni blinks, caught off-guard by the honesty.

Pomni: You’re not-

Jax: Don’t get weird about it.

He raises the pamphlet back up, like a shield.

Jax: Besides, I already did the whole older sibling who knows what they’re doing routine once. Muscle memory or whatever.

Pomni: You really did help raise them, huh.

Jax: Allegedly. Anyways, somebody’s gotta keep you from dropping her.

Pomni: I’m not going to drop her.

Jax: Not with me here, you’re not!

He smiles, small but real.

Pomni: You’re allowed to care… you know.

Jax: No I’m not. It ruins the brand.

Pomni looks down at Daisy’s face.

Pomni: For what it’s worth, I’m kinda glad. I… actually like this part of you.

Jax snorts, but there’s no bite to it.

Jax: Yeah, well… don’t tell anyone. I’ve got a reputation to maintain.

Pomni shifts towards the bassinet.

Pomni: Help me get her down?

Jax is already moving before he answers.

Jax: Yeah, I’ve got her.
