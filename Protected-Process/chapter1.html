Chapter 1

Mother Of The Year

Jax: If I see another Gloink anytime soon, there will be violence.

Jax strolls into the common area. The gang is already present, and all look exhausted from yesterday's adventure.

Zooble: For once, I agree with Jax. What was it, the fourth time we had a Gloinks adventure since Pomni came in? It's getting FUCKING old.

Zooble rolls their eyes.

Pomni: I really don't know why Caine likes to reuse that particular set of NPCs so much.

Gangle: Maybe he's… out of inspiration?

Jax: All I know is that the Queen is the most annoying piece of SHIT I've ever heard.

Ragatha: Well… for what it's worth, all things considered, she's a pretty decent mother.

Jax snorts.

Jax: Oh, here we go again. Rags, just ‘cause you have mommy issues doesn't make every mother you meet a good mom.

Ragatha’s eye widens.

Ragatha: Jax!

Pomni sighs, rubbing her temples.

Pomni: Ragatha does have a point, though. The Gloink Queen, for all her… flaws, is somewhat of a good mother to her spawn. I wouldn't be able to remember all those names.

Jax: You'd make a terrible mom, Pompom.

Pomni: Huh? Why would you-

Caine interrupts.

Caine: GREAT IDEA JAX! We should have an adventure about you being parents!

Pomni: What-

SNAP!

A cloud of multicoloured confetti quickly dissipates around Jax. The gang stares at him, wide-eyed. When he looks down, he realizes he's holding something wrapped in a pastel blue blanket.

It's… a child. A lavender rabbitoid.

Jax: Caine… what the FUCK is this?

Pomni slowly reaches for the newborn.

Pomni: Aww, she's actually pretty cute.

Jax stares at the creature. He lines up a punt, but stops, stares deeper.

It's asleep. It… kinda looks like him, long rabbit ears and soft purple-ish hue. Its forehead fur resembles Pomni's bangs, and it definitely has her blush spots. It's… adorable.

The child curls its tiny paw around his finger. He tries to be angry, but his tail betrays him. One wag, then a few more in rapid succession. His expression softens.

Jax: Maybe… it won't be so bad.

Pomni holds out her hands.

Pomni: Can I?

Jax nods and eases her over. She readjusts the blanket.

Pomni: I've got you now. Easy.

Jax stays close, hands still up, just in case. His tail slows but doesn't stop.

Pomni holds the child the best she can; she's only ever held cousins and nephews before, and never very long.

Jax, somehow, holds it much more naturally.

Ragatha leans in.

Ragatha: So, hmm… what are we naming her?

Pomni and Jax exchange an uncertain look.

Pomni: We… don't know yet.

Caine: Well, I could help with that!

Caine snaps. The familiar name generator drops down from the ceiling. He lines up a swing on the activation lever.

Pomni: No. Absolutely not!

She looks down at the child.

Pomni: Daisy.

Zooble: Daisy? As in Daisy Bell? The song you three belted outside my room to force me out?

Kinger raises a finger.

Kinger: First song a computer ever performed. Bell Labs. Historic.

Pomni: I… think I've read about that somewhere.

Kinger: Read what somewhere?

Gangle: Is that why Caine chose that song? I thought it was a reference to 2001.

Jax: The towers?

Gangle: No, Sheesh. The movie!

Pomni: To be fair, I think that's where Kubrick took the idea.

Kinger: Something about towers?

Jax: Forget it, nutso. It's after your time.

Ragatha: I think it's after mine as well. Did something happen in 2001?

Jax: You… don't wanna know, Rags.

Pomni readjusts the blanket.

Pomni: So, Daisy then?

Jax: Sorry toots! Name’s already taken. Whole tag full of Daisies. You wouldn't believe who it is, either.

Caine pulls out a binder from a non-existent pocket. He riffles through the pages.

Caine: Doesn't look like there's any Daisy in my files.

Jax: Tell that to the search bar!

Zooble: NO. FOURTH WALL. BULLSHIT. We talked about that.

Pomni's voice firms.

Pomni: We're keeping Daisy.

Gangle: It's cute. It suits her.

Jax looks at the kid. His ears soften.

Jax: Yeah. It fits.

Daisy lets out a cute yawn.

Jax: Welcome to the circus, Daisy.

Zooble: So what's the plan here, Caine? These two get to play parents? That's it, that's the adventure?

Caine: Glad you asked, Zooble! This will be a long-term adventure. I haven't done one of those in… well, ever.

Bubble: Except the one time.

Caine: I usually don't like keeping AIs active for prolonged periods of time. It leads to… instability. But, fear not, I'll keep a close eye, or several, on the situation.

Caine snaps his fingers. A pamphlet appears in Jax's hand.

Jax: Oh great… homework. So you've been voluntold to be a parent.

Pomni blinks at the booklet, large pinwheels for pupils.

Pomni: There's a manual?

Caine: A pamphlet. Cheat sheet. It contains all the information you'll need to take proper care of this child.

Gangle: You know, new parents back in the real world get one of those, too. It… doesn't really do a great job preparing you for it.

Jax unfolds it open.

Jax: Feeding schedules, sleep cues, age-acceleration notes… what?

Caine: Daisy will start as a newborn. She’ll age rapidly in order to keep the adventure to a reasonable number of days. Your job is to ensure she's properly cared for.

Ragatha edges closer.

Ragatha: We can help, right?

Caine: Of course! You'll be acting as family and friends to the new parents.

Pomni looks concerned.

Pomni: What if we… can't.

Jax: Waa. It'll be fine, Pompom. I basically raised my younger siblings! Besides, I'm sure Caine will be hovering.

Caine: I'll be monitoring with occasional check-ins. Minimal hand-holding.

Bubble: It's like we're not even here.

Pop.

Zooble: So this never… turns off?

Caine: Not until adolescence. Enough questions!

Snap. Multiple boxes appear nearby, all C&A-branded.

Caine: You've been given a complimentary starter pack. Diapers, bassinet, stroller, everything you need. I'll be providing additional care packages at every milestone.

Caine disappears in a puff of smoke.

The crowd disperses.

Ragatha: We'll be nearby if you need us.

Zooble: I can assemble the stroller, if you want.

Jax: You think we can't-

Pomni: We do. Thanks, Zooble.

Jax sighs.

Pomni gives Zooble a small, relieved smile.

Zooble shrugs, already dragging the box closer.

Zooble: If it collapses, it's user error.

Jax crosses his arms, ears dipping just a little.

Jax: Well, guess that saves me the trouble. Don't mess it up.

His tail gives one quick, traitorous wag. Pomni turns to him.

Pomni: You know, I'm afraid I'll mess up, that I'll do it wrong.

Jax: You will, then you'll get it less wrong.

Pomni looks down at the child.

Pomni: I think… yeah. I… I can work with that.
